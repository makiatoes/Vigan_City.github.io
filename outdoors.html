Mira Hills Fil-Spanish Friendship Park

Mira Hills Fil-Spanish Friendship Park functions as an educational and recreational facility for Vigan residents and tourists. The park attractions include the swimming pool, amphitheatre, Buridek Children's Museum and ChEER Zone. It also has an Eco-Park that is home to a Tree Library. Mira Hills is also the home of the century old water shed of Vigan City.

Plaza Salcedo

DThis historical plaza named after the Spanish conqueror, Juan de Salcedo, is one of the famous tourist attraction in Vigan. It is best visited early in the morning for morning walks/jogs or late afternoon for bonding with family and friends while waiting for the world-class dancing fountain show in the evening.

Baluarte Zoo

The residence of Former Gov. Chavit Singson. He opened it to the public to share his passion for wild life and animals. It has a mini zoo, butterfly garden and an animal encounter show. Food huts and souvenir shops are available in the area.

Plaza Burgos

Plaza Burgos is one of the two major plazas in Vigan. It was named after one of Vigan's illustrious sons, Fr. Jose Burgos. Plaza Burgos is the venue for town fiesta's celebrations and family recreation. Beside the plaza is the Empanadaan where you can taste the famous Vigan Empanada.

Hidden Garden

No one would anticipate that a beautiful garden is situated at the heart of Vigan. Hidden Garden is a place where Filipino hospitality is felt. The garden has a restaurant that serves the famous Vigan empanada and other local delicacies and Filipino food.

Vigan Heritage River Cruise

The Vigan Heritage River Cruise is operated by the City Government of Vigan. It is 35 minute boat ride in the historic Mestizo River. Along the river are life-sized tableau of Vigan history.
