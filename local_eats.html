Vigan Longganisa

The Longganisa or local sausage, is an enduring fusion of Western and Eastern influences dating back from the Colonial Period to the present. It has become an integral fraction of the unique Vigan cultural heritage.

Vigan Bagnet

Vigan Bagnei is the Ilocano version of lechon kawali. Large chunks of pork liempo, belly or pork sides, deep fried for at least two times until crispy. It is best served with tomatoes, onions with bagoong. It can last for 2 months when kept in the freezer.

Vigan Empanada

The mouth-watering Vigan empanada's vegetable filling is made up of grated green papaya or cabbage with whole egg and skinless Vigan longganisa wrapped in rice flour. It is best served with sukang Iloko with "lasona" (Onion.)
